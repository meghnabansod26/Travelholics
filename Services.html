  2. Air Ticket & Commission from Airlines Companies

    3. A. Air travel agents are required to pay 18% GST on commission earned from airlines and also service charges, handling charges etc. (by whatever name called) collected from the customers/ passengers. B. Air travel agents can avail ITC on input services to support the output services of travel agents.

  3. Sale Tour Packages

    4. Tours organized by the tour operator, within India for the tourist coming from abroad is generally known as inbound tours. 2. Tours organised by the tour operators, outside India for the tourist going abroad is generally known as outbound tours.

  4. Travel Related Services like Visa, Passport, etc.

    5. A. VISA processing charges charged by visa statutory authorities will be exempt from GST. B. If visa/ passport is obtained from Visa Facilitation Centers (VFCs) then the same shall be liable to tax

  5. Railway Reservations

    6. A. Rail travel agent are required to pay 18% GST on service charges collected from passengers. B. SAC for Rail travel agent is 9967.
